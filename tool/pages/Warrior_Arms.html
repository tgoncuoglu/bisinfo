    Gear is one of the most important elements in WoW to strengthen your Arms Warrior, providing massive amounts of stats as well as armor, procs, and set bonuses.

    In this guide, we will explain how to obtain the best gear for your Arms Warrior in The War Within Season 1 and how to check if a piece is BiS, an upgrade, or just bad. This guide will help you select the best pieces of gear from Dungeons and Raids in The War Within, whether they be weapons, trinkets, or armor.

        Best Arms Warrior Trinkets in The War Within Season 1

        DPS trinkets tend to be very straightforward, as their damage is not affected by class damage buffs such as Avatar or Colossus Smash. Therefore, direct damage dealing trinkets should be used on cooldown as much as possible, unless aligned with specific boss damage taken increasing mechanics, and stat increasing trinkets should similarly either be used on cooldown or aligned with burst phases.

        Reference Bloodmallet for a list of how each trinket performs at various item levels, but here are important trinkets to be aware of:
        Best Trinkets
        Warriors should typically use one on-use and one passive trinket, or two passive trinkets, but rarely ever two on-use.

        Active Trinkets
        • Treacherous Transmitter is technically the strongest on-use trinket in both single and multitarget content, but requires completing a minor task before receiving the buff. For this reason, the trinket should be activated before using cooldowns, as it will cause a few seconds of distraction, but in worse situations it may be downright impossible to accomplish quickly without interrupting the rest of your rotation, reducing or eliminating any potential gains. It is therefore strongly advised to only use the trinket if you are very comfortable with both it and the encounter/content its being used in.
        • Mad Queen's Mandate is a strong single target effect, though with the downside of being on the global cooldown and requiring a short cast time. Despite this, it is still a powerful option that is easier to use than the Transmitter, though care should be taken to activate it outside of major cooldowns, though the upside to this is a natural desync with other on-use trinkets.
        • Overclocked Gear-a-Rang Launcher is a boomerang-like skillshot best used with a cursor macro to aim behind the target, in order to ensure it hits while moving away and returning.
        • Mark of Khardros is a weaker but viable alternative to the Transmitter, but without the gimmick, allowing it to be macrod together with other burst cooldowns as a convenient "fire and forget" option that is particularly good for Mythic+ and multitarget content. Skarmorak Shard is virtually identical, offering 5% less Mastery on-use in exchange for a smaller amount whenever an enemy dies.
        Passive Trinkets
        • Ara-Kara Sacbrood is generally the best passive trinket, with the majority of its value coming from the stat bonus. The spawned spiderling tis somewhat gimmicky due to the time they take to hatch making them less viable for high movement dungeons and raid encounters, but typically contributes <1% damage anyway. As there are very few completely passive trinkets available at higher item levels in Season 1, this is highly recommended.

        Best Items to Upgrade for Arms Warriors

        When upgrading gear in The War Within, Warriors should typically prioritize weapons, trinkets, and special effect items due to their having the highest impact on performance, though this may be out of reach in the first weeks of the season, so simply prioritizing upgrades to your lowest item level pieces of gear may be preferred. Remember too that while many crafted embellishment items are powerful, they are not as efficient to upgrade as regular pieces of gear.

        Best Crafted Gear for Arms Warriors

        Dragonflight adds many new options for crafted gear, allowing players to craft even Mythic-equivalent gear! You'll generally want to supplement gear from Raids and Mythic+ with crafted pieces. Note that crafting is limited by Spark of Awakening. After receiving your first Spark of Awakening from the first week of The War Within Season 1, you will receive another Spark of Awakening every other week while The War Within Season 1 is active.

          Best Embellishments

          Crafting items with Embellishments gives them special properties and effects in addition to regular stats. However, you may only wear 2 pieces of gear with Embellishments at a time. With that in mind, the best Embellishments are:
          • Duskthread Lining is a strong all-round option for Arms, being competitive in raid and easy to maintain in Mythic. It's also more forgiving than the Dragonflight version, having an 80% health threshold instead of 90%. Dawnthread Lining is a similar option, but with Arms Warriors already stacking Critical Strike on gear, its value is diminished.
          • Darkmoon Sigil: Ascension is tied or very slightly behind double Duskthread depending on its uptime, but may only be applied to weapons, which requires two crafting sparks instead of one. It is also very difficult to maintain in Mythic+ due to the short timeframe in which it checks for combat.
          • Writhing Armor Banding doubles the value of the Darkmoon Sigil weapon embellishments, while being crafted on a lower budget piece (wrist/cloak). Because this will be paired with a Darkmoon embellished 2h weapon requiring two sparks, the Banding can be crafted onto a rare 590 item level piece instead, avoiding the need for an extra spark at the start of the season and upgraded to a higher item level version later on.

          Recommended Crafting Order

          Crafting order is largely a matter of preference or based on the needs of current gearing, but with so many items purchasable and upgradeable, there aren't a lot of gear slots to choose from. For that reason, simply prioritizing the embellishments for your chosen content type is the best course of action. Because Arms has a single set of universal embellishments good for all content, there isn't much to consider here, although early progression players concerned with the availability of weapons may wish to consider crafting a weapon instead.
          1. Consecrated Cloak
          2. Everforged Vambraces
          3. Any low item level piece of gear.
          With the addition of the Magnificent Jeweler's Setting, crafting early rings for extra sockets is no longer advised, and because the maximum crafted item level is also slightly lower than upgradeable raid or mythic+ gear, the best crafted items are prioritized on the lowest stat budget pieces of gear.
