    Gear is one of the most important elements in WoW to strengthen your Restoration Shaman, providing massive amounts of stats as well as armor, procs, and set bonuses.

    In this guide, we will explain how to obtain the best gear for your Restoration Shaman in The War Within Season 1 and how to check if a piece is BiS, an upgrade, or just bad. This guide will help you select the best pieces of gear from Dungeons and Raids in The War Within, whether they be weapons, trinkets, or armor.

     Recommended Gear for Restoration Shaman in The War Within Season 1

    What is the BiS Restoration Shaman gear?

    With many different sources of gearing and loot scarcity in The War Within, targeting your absolute Best in Slot gear isn't entirely realistic; instead, we've created a Best in Slot list from the ideal Restoration Shaman drops found in The War Within Season 1 from Mythic+ Dungeons and Raids to help you decide what to prioritize.
        As a rough guideline follow the stat priority of: Critical Strike > Versatility > Mastery >= Haste. Don't neglect the value of Intellect, a substantial increase in ilvl is generally worth it compared to optimized stats. It is always recommended that you use QuestionablyEpic to sim your own character since every character has different circumstances.

         Best Restoration Shaman Trinkets in The War Within Season 1

        Restoration Shamans are mainly looking for one powerful on-use trinket to pair with their cooldowns, and a supplementary either proc or flat stat trinket. The stats Restoration is most looking for on trinkets are Critical Strike and Versatility.

        • Gale of ShadowsExplanation

          This is another solid stat-stick where multiple stacks "overlap". This seems especially nice in a Mythic+ setting, as the damage procs also add in a small amount of damage.
        • Unbound ChangelingExplanation
          This is a unique stat-stick that allows you to customize which secondary stat it procs by the food that you eat. This makes it very versatile, and it's also fairly easy to get since it comes from Mythic+.
        A Tier
        • Empowering Crystal of Anub'ikkajExplanation
          This is your stereotypical stat-stick. Unfortunately this gives you a random secondary stat, so you can have streaks of "bad" secondary stat procs. Not terrible, not great.
        • Ovi'nax's Mercurial EggExplanation
          This is a unique take on a passive stat-stick trinket. Standing still will build up stacks of Deliberate Incubation which increases your mainstat over time, and moving will remove stacks of Deliberate Incubation and instead grant stacks of Reckless Incubation. This means you will never get zero value from this trinket, but it does award standing still as mainstat is generally better. Using the trinket will "suspend" the procs you have for a period of time, making it quite versatile.

         Best Items to Upgrade for Restoration Shaman

        Completing endgame activities in The War Within will award you with Harbinger Crests and Valorstones. You can use these currencies to upgrade items you obtain to their max potential (explained in this guide). Due to the more powerful items requiring the limited currency of Crests on a weekly lockout, it means your decision making early on can be difficult. In general, you want to prioritize items you've obtained at the highest upgrade track you can obtain, with the following criteria to decide on the item:
        1. Main Hand WeaponExplanation
          This is your first priority to get high item level, but you do have an alternative option in crafting if you wish. If you get hold of a good enough option here, it may be better to do this and switch your Spark of Omens priority accordingly.
        2. Strong TrinketsExplanation
          If you happen to get either Spymaster's Web or Ovi'nax's Mercurial Egg at a Heroic or Mythic version, these are quite solid trinkets that you're unlikely to replace for quite awhile. If you're only doing Mythic+, getting either Gale of Shadows or Unbound Changeling on Heroic or Myth track are great options to upgrade, as they'll be quite rare to see in your Great Vault.
        3. Lowest Item Level Pieces Explanation
          Following key pieces, bringing up the item level of pieces you don't expect to get hold of at a higher item level in the near future to remove the Crest requirement is best. Ideally you should do Helm, Chest and Legs first due to their stat budget, but you can always double-check with QE Live

         Best Crafted Gear for Restoration Shaman

        Recommended Crafting Order

        Please see the notes below each of the sections, especially when crafting a 2-handed weapon. Make sure to not craft a strength or agility weapon.
        1. A Two-Handed Weapon with Darkmoon Sigil: Ascension or Darkmoon Sigil: Symbiosis on it. Please read notes below
          • If you are killing Mythic bosses or clearing M+9s on the second week of the season: Do not craft week 1 (Heroic Week). Instead, wait for week 2 (Mythic Week) when we have access to Enchanted Gilded Harbinger Crest because this is the highest possible value for your crests.
          • If you are only clearing Heroic Raid or only do medium-level keys at the start of the season: You likely wont have enough Gilded Crests to make Enchanted Gilded Harbinger Crest, so use Enchanted Runed Harbinger Crest on your weapon craft instead.
          • If you are only ever going to do Mythic+ and have no intentions of raiding: be aware that Darkmoon Sigil: Ascension is worse in Mythic+ because the stacks regularly fall off. This is still fine to use for an early power spike, but you will likely replace it with Duskthread Lining later in the season. Additionally, if you feel as though a 1-handed weapon and a shield is necessary, craft your main-hand weapon first followed by a shield for your first 2 sparks using the same rules as above.
          • Double Check you are not crafting a Strength or Agility Weapon.
        2. Craft Writhing Armor Banding on either bracers or cape. For the start of the season, keeping these on a ilvl 590 piece to start is fine, and you can upgrade them later when we have access to more Spark of Omens. The idea here is we craft a very valuable high-budget slot like a Weapon with Enchanted Gilded Harbinger Crest and then double the effect with Writhing Armor Banding on a low-budget slot for an early season power spike.
        3. Any Low Item Level Piece
          • After getting priority crafts, use Spark of Omens to fill holes in your gear to increase your overall item level. You can also augment any remaining embellishment slots with Dawnthread Lining or Duskthread Lining if you're not already at 2 embellishments.
          • It's best to simulate your character using the QE Live to pick out good upgrades.

        Reinforced Setae Flyers and Busy Bee's Buckle - This set is currently very undertuned and requires you to often go out of your way to collect the orbs of pollen that occasionally spawn. For now, this effect is simply too annoying to be worthwhile, and also takes up 2 of your valuable Spark of Omens.
        Sanctified Torchbearer's Grips - This might be something you opt for if your goal is strictly to add in more damage, but this does take up a valuable tier slot. The damage they do is currently quite low, so I'd only recommend trying this if the rest of your gear is in a very good state. This is something that would only potentially be useful for Mythic+ only.
        Whirring Wristwraps - (Mythic+*) The tinker slot provided by this lets us get access to Tinker: Arclight Vital Correctors , so the utility of having a combat res when we can use Reincarnation ourselves is extremely valuable for these situations. Use a Meticulously-Tuned Gear or Rapidly Ticking Gear and Calibrated Safety Switch when crafting.

        Your Spark of Omens items are required for crafting some of the most powerful The War Within equipment, so you should spend them wisely, aiming to craft them at the highest possible item level. Note that you can always use the recrafting system later down the line if you want to upgrade pieces, so there's no need to hoard them if there's something big you can get now. Much like early in Dragonflight, these are time-gated, with you receiving one every two weeks, and can be upgraded all the way up to Item Level 636.

        While there is a section below regarding Embellishments, your Spark of Omens crafts should aim to craft the highest impact item that's least likely to see a replacement from other sources due to their scarcity. Every craft you perform should aim to use a Algari Missive of the Quickblade to guarantee Crit and Versatility, unless the piece has fixed stats already present or your stat balance leans you toward a different recommendation.

         Best Embellishments

        Using the optional Embellishment items when crafting your gear provides small extra benefits to the item should the craft be successful. These effects are often quite minor and mostly considered a cherry on top, your decision on what to craft should (generally) be dictated by the slot rather than these effects with some small exceptions. They are limited to two, so putting one on for your early crafts is free added performance. Details about each are as follows:
        1. Darkmoon Sigil: Ascension or Darkmoon Sigil: Symbiosis Explanation
          Getting high item-level weapons early on is very important, and we're also able to put an embellished effect such as these sigils on our main-hand weapon to give us a bunch of extra stats. You need to pretty consistently be in combat, so this is better in a Raid than it is in Mythic+, but still a good chunk of stats early on in the season.
        2. Any Low Item Level PieceExplanation
          You're able to augment almost any crafted piece of gear with an embellished effect of some type, and almost all of the embellishments are currently very underwhelming. It is better to simply fill in lower item-level slots and filling them in with any embellished effect, rather than aiming for one of the specific embellishments. You can also augment any remaining embellishment slots with Dawnthread Lining or Duskthread Lining if you're not already at 2 embellishments.
        3. Binding of BindingExplanation
          This ring is okay if you're regularly running with the same group and you also care about the stats that it's giving your bonded partner, however it is not very good for personal throughput.
