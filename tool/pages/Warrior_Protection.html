    Gear is one of the most important elements in WoW to strengthen your Protection Warrior, providing massive amounts of stats as well as armor, procs, and set bonuses.

    In this guide, we will explain how to obtain the best gear for your Protection Warrior in The War Within Season 1 and how to check if a piece is BiS, an upgrade, or just bad. This guide will help you select the best pieces of gear from Dungeons and Raids in The War Within, whether they be weapons, trinkets, or armor.

    Recommended Gear for Protection Warrior in The War Within Season 1

    What is the BiS Protection Warrior gear?

    With many different sources of gearing and loot scarcity in The War Within, targeting your absolute Best in Slot gear isn't entirely realistic; instead, we've created a Best in Slot list from the ideal Protection Warrior drops found in The War Within Season 1 from Mythic+ Dungeons and Raids to help you decide what to prioritize.
        Increasing your item level is generally more important than optimizing your secondary stats. If you are comparing pieces of the same item level, then you should follow the simple stat priority of Haste > Crit >= Versatility > Mastery.

        It is always recommended that you use Raidbots to sim your own character, since every character is different.

        Defensively there is very little difference that stats will have on your overall success in any scenario. Your personal play matters a lot more than trying to shift around a handful of percent. So don't read too much into the gearing progress.

        Best Protection Warrior Trinkets in The War Within Season 1

        Protection Warriors are tanky by default, so adding more offensive value through trinkets is usually a good idea. You can boost your defenses even further if need be, but there are considerable diminishing returns in getting tankier. Damage is always good!

        Best Items to Upgrade for Protection Warriors

        In general, getting weapons, shields, and trinkets at the maximum item level you can is the best use of your gear upgrade currencies. The difference between a well-itemized weapon and a poorly-itemized weapon is pretty small, whereas the difference between a high-item-level weapon and a low-item-level weapon is substantial, so just upgrade whatever weapon and shield you have available.

        After that, focus on trinkets, particularly those near the top of the above list, and then just try to upgrade your lowest item-level piece of gear as much as you can.
        1. ShieldExplanation
          This is your first priority to get a high item level, but you do have an alternative option in crafting if you wish.
        2. Strong TrinketsExplanation
          Thankfully our best trinkets this time around are m+ drops. So these should be easier than normal to obtain and can result in a nice bump in player power!
        3. Lowest Item Level Pieces Explanation
          Following key pieces, bringing up the item level of pieces you don't expect to get hold of at a higher item level in the near future to remove the Crest requirement is best. Ideally, you should do Helm, Chest, and Legs first due to their stat budget, but after that go off Sims.

        Recommended Crafting Order

        You will want to use crafted gear to fill in your weakest overall gear spots whenever possible, so take the list below as a suggestion if all other things are equal, rather than a hard-and-fast requirement.
        1. Duskthread Lining x2 – Fill in your weakest Armor pieces, this can be used on Everforged Defender.
        2. Everforged Defender
        3. Jewelery are always good pieces to have completed due to lack of Strength and Armor!

        Crafting Notes

        • Beledar's Bulwark
          Beledar's Bulwark
        • Sanctified Steps
          Sanctified Steps
        Beledar's Bulwark - While one would think that a shield that grants a versatility proc sounds good, it sadly has quite low uptime on the proc itself which does get outclassed by other embellishments.
        Sanctified Steps - In the best-case scenario these boots reach 10 stacks in a little over a minute making it rather weak of an embellishment compared to consistent stat uptimes. Can't recommend it.

        Your Spark of Omens items are required for crafting some of the most powerful The War Within equipment, so you should spend them wisely, aiming to craft them at the highest possible item level. Note as well that you can always use the recrafting system later down the line if you want to upgrade pieces, so there's no need to hoard them if there's something big you can get now.

        While there is a section below regarding Embellishments, your Spark of Omens crafts should aim to craft the highest impact item that's least likely to see a replacement from other sources due to their scarcity. Every craft you perform should aim to use a Algari Missive of the Fireflash to guarantee Haste and Critical Strike, unless the piece has fixed stats already present or your stat balance leans you toward a different recommendation.

        Best Embellishments

        Crafting items with Embellishments gives them special properties and effects in addition to regular stats. However, you may only wear 2 pieces of gear with Embellishments at a time. With that in mind, the best Embellishments are:
        1. Dawnthread Lining or Duskthread Lining x2 depending on gear
        2. Elemental Focusing Lens x2 for pure single target damage
