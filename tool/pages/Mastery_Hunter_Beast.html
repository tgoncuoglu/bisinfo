    Gear is one of the most important elements in WoW to strengthen your Beast Mastery Hunter, providing massive amounts of stats as well as armor, procs, and set bonuses.

    In this guide, we will explain how to obtain the best gear for your Beast Mastery Hunter in The War Within Season 1 and how to check if a piece is BiS, an upgrade, or just bad. This guide will help you select the best pieces of gear from Dungeons and Raids in The War Within, whether they be weapons, trinkets, or armor.

    Recommended Gear for Beast Mastery Hunter in The War Within Season 1

    What is the BiS Beast Mastery Hunter gear?

    With many different sources of gearing and loot scarcity in The War Within, targeting your absolute Best in Slot gear isn't entirely realistic; instead, we've created a Best in Slot list from the ideal Beast Mastery Hunter drops found in The War Within Season 1 from Mythic+ Dungeons and Raids to help you decide what to prioritize.
        Below you have a best-in-slot list made for raid content and Mythic+ respectively. Two lists are required as your stat priority is vastly different between the two forms of content. For the raid list we'll be using the priority of: Haste>Crit>Mastery, while for the Mythic+ list, we'll be using the priority of: Mastery>Crit=Haste. Agility and Versatility are the bottom two priorities in either content. This could mean lower item level items could beat higher item level items, if they have better secondary stats. You should always aim to sim yourself to figure out the best setup for your particular scenario. For Pack Leader, you can simply use the "Mythic+" list for Raid too.

        Best Items to Upgrade for Beast Mastery Hunter

        Completing endgame activities in The War Within will award you with Harbinger Crests and Valorstones. You can use these currencies to upgrade items you obtain to their max potential (explained in this guide). Due to the more powerful items requiring the limited currency of Crests on a weekly lockout, it means your decision making early on can be difficult. In general, you want to prioritize items you've obtained at the highest upgrade track you can obtain, with the following criteria to decide on the item:
        1. Your Best TrinketsExplanation
          Skardyn's Grace and Ara-Kara Sacbrood are your strongest trinkets for all forms of content. It therefore makes a lot of sense to upgrade them, as they'll see plenty of use.
        2. Highly statted items you will likely not replace Explanation
          This is generally speaking Tier Items, especially Legs, Chest and Head. Rings and necklaces can also fall into this category if they have very good stats for your goals.
        3. Lowest Item Level Pieces Explanation
          This is primarily to remove the crest requirement in the future. Remember you don't have to spend your crest if there is nothing exciting to upgrade at the moment!

        Best Crafted Gear for Beast Mastery Hunter

        Your Spark of Omens items are required for crafting some of the most powerful The War Within equipment, so you should spend them wisely, aiming to craft them at the highest possible item level. Note that you can always use the recrafting system later down the line if you want to upgrade pieces, so there's no need to hoard them if there's something big you can get now. Much like early in Dragonflight, these are time-gated, with you receiving one every two weeks, and can be upgraded all the way up to Item Level 636.

        While there is a section below regarding Embellishments, your Spark of Omens crafts should aim to craft the highest impact item that's least likely to see a replacement from other sources due to their scarcity. If your focus is on raid content, your crafted items should be statted with Haste and Crit using Algari Missive of the Fireflash , while if your focus is on Mythic+, they should be statted with Mastery and Crit or Haste using Algari Missive of the Peerless or Algari Missive of the Feverflare .

          Recommended Crafting Order

          Here you'll find the recommended order of crafting gear:
          1. Glyph-Etched Vambraces with Dawnthread Lining .
            • The strongest single-target embellishment on a weak gear slot.
          2. Glyph-Etched Gauntlets with Dawnthread Lining .
            • The strongest single-target embellishment, again. The slot here is flexible but gloves is a weak slot due to high levels of Versatility on all raid and Mythic+ gloves.
          3. Low item level slots
            • Whatever you've been unlucky with looting.

          Best Embellishments

          Below you'll find your three strongest embellishments. Embellishments outside of these are generally not worth considering, but note that the difference is quite small.
          1. Dawnthread Lining *Explanation
            Considered with at least 60% uptime. Decent crit buff at reasonable uptime.
          2. Darkmoon Sigil: Ascension *Explanation
            Solid random buff to a random secondary stat.
          3. Elemental Focusing Lens *Explanation
            Decent damage proc.
